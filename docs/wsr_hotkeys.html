Alt-l  ... lowercase selected

The only hot-keys that do not have buttons associated with them are:
  • Ctrl-c (copy text)
  • Ctrl-v (paste text)
  • Ctrl-z & t (undo & redo)
  • Ctrl-ESC (clear found text)
  • Ctrl-h (help)
Many of the buttons do not have hot-keys associated with them. Button are labeled with their hot-key when hovered.
